Employment Hysteresis from the Great Recession (JPE forthcoming)

  • Hysteresis Data Table 2 (Stata, Excel): Blanchard-Katz Replication Data - Employment Statistics by State and Year
  • Hysteresis Data Table 3 (Stata, Excel): Age-Adjusted U.S. Employment Statistics by Year
  • Hysteresis Data Table 4 (Stata, Excel): U.S. Employment Statistics by Month
  • Hysteresis Data Table 5 (Stata, Excel): U.S. Employment Statistics by Age and Year
  • Hysteresis Data Table 6 (Stata, Excel): Employment Statistics by Commuting Zone and Month
